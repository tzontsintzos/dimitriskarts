intimate

Intimate is a photographic series that employs self-portraiture as a reflection of personal moments and emotions. The use of red lighting plays a central role, symbolizing intense psychological states such as love and anger. By abstracting the body, the series explores how fragments of the figure emerge and recede, suggesting that intimacy lies not in the completeness of form, but in the tension between what is revealed and what remains hidden.

attached body

Attached Body is a dialogue between body and landscape, where self-portraiture becomes an act of return to origin. By repositioning the body within nature, the work seeks intimacy with the land, blurring the boundary between human form and environment.

Claustrophobic framings overwhelm the image with detail, mirroring states of fear and inner turbulence, while the harsh light sculpts the body into a living monument. Through this process, Attached Body transforms into a meditation on vulnerability, harmony, and the search for belonging within nature. The abstract body depiction connotes the exploration of the mode a fragment could represent a whole.

FLORA Scarf Collection

FLORA the Scarf Collection was created by the idea of using photographs of flowers that I took, turning them into timeless pieces. FLORA is a wearable archive of nature. Photographs of flowers are transformed into patterns that move beyond the frame, reimagined as timeless scarves. Each piece becomes both an image and an object, carrying traces of fragility, vibrancy, and growth. Through this transformation, FLORA explores how photography can shift from a medium of observation into one of embodiment turning the act of seeing into the act of wearing.

analogue diary

Analogue Diary is a nostalgic outtake on life, begun at the age of sixteen as a way to preserve fleeting moments. Through the grain and imperfections of film, the series captures fragments of places, objects, and atmospheres that resonate with memory. Each photograph becomes a quiet entr, not a record of events, but a tender reflection on time, loss, and the persistence of beauty in the ordinary

ABOUT

PHOTOGRAPHER, VISUAL ARTIST
BASED IN ATHENS, GR

BORN AND RAISED IN SOUTHERN GREECE, A PHOTOGRAPHY AND AUDIOVISUAL ARTS GRADUATE FROM THE UNIVERSITY OF WEST ATTICA. MY WORK MOVES BETWEEN ADVERTISING, FASHION, AND INTERIOR ARCHITECTURE, WHERE I EXPERIMENT WITH FORM, TEXTURE, AND LIGHT TO CREATE IMAGES THAT ARE BOTH INTIMATE AND CONCEPTUAL. PASSIONATE ABOUT ANALOG PROCESSES, I SEEK TO MERGE ARTISTIC EXPERIMENTATION WITH CONTEMPORARY VISUAL CULTURE, SHAPING PHOTOGRAPHY AS BOTH A PERSONAL LANGUAGE AND A COLLABORATIVE MEDIUM.
