ABOUT

PHOTOGRAPHER, VISUAL ARTIST
BASED IN ATHENS, GR

BORN AND RAISED IN SOUTHERN GREECE, A PHOTOGRAPHY AND AUDIOVISUAL ARTS GRADUATE FROM THE UNIVERSITY OF WEST ATTICA. MY WORK MOVES BETWEEN ADVERTISING, FASHION, AND INTERIOR ARCHITECTURE, WHERE I EXPERIMENT WITH FORM, TEXTURE, AND LIGHT TO CREATE IMAGES THAT ARE BOTH INTIMATE AND CONCEPTUAL. PASSIONATE ABOUT ANALOG PROCESSES, I SEEK TO MERGE ARTISTIC EXPERIMENTATION WITH CONTEMPORARY VISUAL CULTURE, SHAPING PHOTOGRAPHY AS BOTH A PERSONAL LANGUAGE AND A COLLABORATIVE MEDIUM.
